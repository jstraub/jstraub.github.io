LAMP lifts detected 2D body keypoints from all cameras into a unified 3D world
reference frame using known device pose and calibration, then fits 3D human
motion directly to this 3D ray cloud with a spatio-temporal transformer. This
"lift-then-fit" approach learns to track world-space human motion and achieves
state-of-the-art results on monocular benchmarks while significantly
outperforming baselines in the egocentric setting.
