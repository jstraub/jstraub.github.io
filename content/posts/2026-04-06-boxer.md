BoxerNet is a transformer that lifts 2D bounding box proposals into 3D given
gravity direction and optional sparse or dense depth. The Boxer system fuses
and filters the per-frame 3D bounding boxes incrementally across multiple views
to produce globally consistent, de-duplicated 3D boxes in metric world space.
Leveraging off-the-shelf 2D detectors lets BoxerNet focus on lifting rather
than detecting, allowing Boxer to detect open-world 3D objects while reducing
the need for costly annotated 3D box training data.
