BoxerNet lifts 2D bounding box proposals to 3D given gravity and optional sparse
or dense depth, and Boxer fuses them across views into globally consistent,
de-duplicated 3D boxes in metric world space. Building on off-the-shelf 2D
detectors, it localizes open-world objects in 3D.
