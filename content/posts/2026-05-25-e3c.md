E3C generates controllable, physically grounded egocentric video by disentangling
persistent scene structure from human-driven dynamics. It builds a semi-dense
point-cloud 3D memory of the environment and renders ego- and exo-skeletons for
human pose control, improving visual fidelity, camera-motion accuracy, object
consistency, and human control.
