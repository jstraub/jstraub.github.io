ShapeR generates high-fidelity 3D shapes from casually captured image sequences.
It uses SLAM, 3D detection, and vision-language models to extract per-object conditioning
and a rectified flow transformer to generate shapes, achieving 2.7x improvement in Chamfer
distance over state of the art.
